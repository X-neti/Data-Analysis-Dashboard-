## ReadMe
## Project Objective
1. Think from the perspective of Business Leaders
2. Identify visual which would explain the data and Insight in the simplest possible manner
3. Create the visuals around CEO and CMO requested questions using PowerBi

## Exploratory Data Analysis
1. Create a monthly revenue time series for 2011 to identify seasonal trends and support forecasting for upcoming year
2. Visualize the top 10 Countries by revenue and quantity excluding the United Kingdom
3. Identify the top 10 Customers by revenue in descending order to retain Higher-Value Customers
4. Map a global demand for products excluding the United Kingdom to identify Market expansion opportunities

## Process
Data Cleaning: Some returns to the store are provided in negative quantities and some unit prices where below $0
1. Created a check that the quantity should not be below 1 unit.
2. Created a check that the unit price should not be below $0.

Data Visualization: 
Applied filters in Power Bi to show only the relevant information to the CEO and CMO.

## Project Insight
1. Filtered unit price and quantity to remove negatives.
2. The Q4 surge: Revenue remains stable for the first 8 months but begins a sharp ascent in September with November being the highest earning month of 2011.
3. While the United Kingdom is the primary market, the Netherland and Ireland (EIRE) also generated high revenue and transactions.
4. The top 10 customers reveals a significant contribution to company’s revenue.

## Final Conclusion
1. The business is currently in a strong position for geographic expansion within Europe, targeting specifically the Netherlands and Ireland (EIRE).
2. To maximize ROI, marketing efforts should be doubled with the seasonal surge in Q4 and loyalty programs should be implemented to protect the revenue for top 1% of customer base.
